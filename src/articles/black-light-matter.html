Black "light" matter

Black "light" matter is an entirely new and undiscovered form of matter derived from light,

which explains the absence of holy radiation in black holes. It's a new kind of non-atomic matter. We have developed technology for producing black light energy in a dormant state. This is achievable through our innovations in accumulating energy in cosmic bubbles that can remain as matter. We aim to convert light energy into matter through quantum technologies and subsequently plan to transform other radiations into matter. We aim to open a new chapter in understanding matter.

Black "light" matter is a significantly potent alternative to all existing forms of energy. However, we do not seek to own this technology, which requires significant financial investments. If anyone is interested in owning this new matter, we value it at 5 billion dollars per sample, with a prepayment for its production.
