Relevance and value of the product Space bubbles

Having studied a number of scientific papers, as well as having conducted a series of our own experiments, our team came to the conclusion that at this stage of development,science remains at the same place in many areas of astronautics and astrophysics.

For more than half a century, the conquest of outer space continues to be predominant not only in the scientific but also in the cultural and social paradigm of mankind's quest.

Having studied a number of scientific papers, as well as having conducted a series of our own experiments, our team came to the conclusion that at this stage of development, science remains at the same place in many areas of astronautics and astrophysics, the key of which are :

  • significant energy consumption of most types of engines of modern spacecraft;
  • the absence of real, inexhaustible sources of energy that could be used for both "space" and "earth" purposes;
  • the absence of mechanisms that could ensure the movement of micro-and macro space vehicles at the speed of the solar wind in outer space to other galaxies;
  • lack of effective, practical measures for the destruction of space debris;
  • insufficient preventive effectiveness of modern methods of protecting the Earth from asteroids and other celestial bodies;
  • the limitations of modern space (solar) sails, as well as of other research devices that use the pressure of sunlight or a laser onto the mirror surface to propel spacecraft;
  • lack of proper conditions for creating a thermonuclear reaction on Earth;
  • Material and technical limitations of already existing projects for the creation of research bases and autonomous settlements for human work and life on other planets. Limitations in real projects when creating bases for humans on other planets.
