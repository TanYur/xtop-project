Meditation in a Virtual 3D Space

Our project represents a technological upgrade of meditative practices, aimed at regenerating the human body at the cellular level, fostering mindfulness, and aiding in the internal engineering of oneself. To achieve this, we aim to utilize software in the format of a 3D virtual space, capable of recreating yogic meditative practices where individuals visualize specific images in their imagination. Our goal is to manifest these images through three-dimensional visualization.

We also aim to influence the state of the meditating individual by creating specific zones of warmth or light of a certain spectrum in particular areas of the body, as well as scents and tactile sensations. During virtual meditation, we will track breathing patterns, as well as the hormonal, physical, and chemical parameters of the individual, allowing us to adjust meditation parameters to enhance the meditator's state.
