Revolution in 3D Modeling of the Face and Posture:

Xttopbio Introduces Innovative Technology

In the era of rapid technological advancement, Xttopbio makes a significant contribution to the field of medical innovations by introducing cutting-edge 3D modeling and preservation technology for the face and posture. Our mission is to provide a unique solution that preserves the natural beauty and individuality of the face and posture throughout one's entire life.

Exclusive Assurance of Facial Preservation

Xttopbio offers a unique guarantee of preserving the face and neck for up to 60 years using our technology. This marks a revolutionary step in the cosmetic industry, providing patients with the opportunity to maintain their natural appearance despite the aging process.

Artificial Details for Unprecedented Realism

In some cases, we propose adding innovative modules that allow the application of artificial color for the skin and artistic designs. This opens up new possibilities for expressing individuality and creativity in the fashion industry.

Virtual Avatar and Change Tracking

We integrate our technology with a virtual avatar of the individual, providing a unique system for tracking changes in the face and body. This enables us to dynamically adapt to any alterations and maintain a natural appearance throughout a person's life.

Specialized Software for Convenience

To ensure maximum convenience and efficiency in using our technology, we are developing specialized software. This software will be tailored to the needs of our users, ensuring ease of management and maintenance of the desired external appearance.

Together into the Future of Beauty and Innovation

Xttopbio aims to be a pioneer in 3D modeling of the face and posture, introducing innovations that will reshape perceptions of beauty and possibilities for preserving natural aesthetics. We are confident that our technology will become a key element in the evolution of medical practices, offering users a new level of self-confidence and comfort.
