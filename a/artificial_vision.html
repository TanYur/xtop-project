Artificial Vision.

Problem: The challenge we aim to solve concerns individuals who have difficulty seeing or are completely blind. Their limited vision affects their everyday lives, limiting their independence and opportunities. Existing solutions don't offer a complete substitute for natural sight.

Solution: Our solution offers a novel way to simulate vision for people with visual impairments. It involves a discreet wearable device placed on the user's forehead. This device captures information about the surrounding environment through specialized sensors.

This information is processed by advanced software, creating a representation of the world that is conveyed to the user through non-visual means. Users can perceive and interpret this representation, allowing them to gain a better understanding of their surroundings.

The solution promotes user engagement and independence without relying on traditional visual cues. It is a promising step towards improving the lives of individuals with visual impairments.
